OUR VISION

OWNX aims to develop OWNerverse - a community focused virtual world with its own decentralized economy powered by OWNX token. The OWNerverse community members will be empowered to acquire their OWN digital assets, create experiences and monetize them.
We are developing a new approach to the users' experience and OWNership.

White Paper

OWNERVERSE

OWNerverse consists of 17 floating islands that resemble the shape of OWNX brand logo. The islands are divided into 3 sections, each with its own theme and purpose.
1- Twelve floating islands are composed in the shape of hexagon and will serve mainly as residential area. Community members will be enabled to acquire plots of land, become LandOwners and monetize their assets. 
2- Four larger islands are composed in the shape of “X” symbol situated in the middle of the hexagon. These “4 Seasons” themed islands will serve mainly as leisure hubs, where community members will get access to a variety of entertainment and social activities.
       Summerland 
A tropical paradise, a gateway to leisure and entertainment facilities including hotels, aqua park, amusement park, casino, clubs; the island will offer a wide range of summer activities in a resort-alike environment. 
        Winterland
An ultimate winter destination, snow-covered island surrounded by white hills, mountain slopes fir trees, chalets and igloo buildings; the Island will provide a numerous winter activities including skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, sledding and many more.
       Mountainland
Away from the hustle and bustle of the dOWN-tOWN, this orange-hued sandy land surrounded by mountains and dunes, offers a perfect nature retreat and adventurous experiences including desert safari, quad biking, dune bashing and even hot air balloon rides.
      Sportland
Designed to be the OWNerverse ultimate sport and leisure destination, the Sportland will host major sporting events and championships.

  3- dOWN-tOWN - the central island located in the heart of the OWNerverse. Serving as a hub for business, retail, leisure and entertainment facilities is divided into several districts:
- Central Business District featuring high concentration of offices, companies headquarters, banks, financial institutions and so on.
- Cultural District featuring NFT Art Gallery (The Museum), performing art center, theater.
- Leisure & Entertainment District featuring parks, music hall, club, cinema.
- Shopping District featuring the Shopping Mall, freestanding boutiques, caffe shops and restaurants.
- Knowledge District featuring education center, exhibition center, the Tech Labs.
- Sport District featuring the sport complex, the Arena.
- Residential District featuring high-end residential community, waterfront villas and townhouses.

OWNX MARKETPLACE

OWNX Marketplace is a decentralized platform managed by smart contracts. All offers and digital assets that can be traded within the OWNerverse will be available in the Marketplace for purchase. All transactions will be handled in OWNX tokens.
Types of Trade:
1. Digital Assets.
OWNerverse community members will be enabled to buy, sell or rent digital assets using OWNX Marketplace features.
2. Experiences.
In addition to the multiple attractions available within the OWNerverse, LandOWNErs will be empowered to monetize their digital assets by creating new experiences and offer them in the Marketplace for other members to avail.
3. P2P Services and Offers/ Jobs
Experienced professionals will be enabled to monetize their skills by offering services through the Marketplace, whilst those willing to monetize their digital assets (the OWNErs) will be able to find for right candidates for their assets development.
4. Offers and products from affiliated partners/brands
Community members will get access to offers and products from OWNX affiliated partners and brands.
